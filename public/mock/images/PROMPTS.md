# Mock Product Image Prompts

This document provides high-quality prompts designed for image generation models (like Midjourney, DALL-E, or Gemini Image) to create a consistent, premium aesthetic for the POS mock data.

## Aesthetics Guidelines

- **Lighting**: Soft morning natural light, cinematic shadows.
- **Background**: Minimalist cafe setting, blurred wooden textures, marble counters, or warm bokeh.
- **Style**: High-end food photography, 8k resolution, sharp focus, vibrant but natural colors.
- **Consistency**: No text, no logos, no people (focus solely on the product).

---

### 1. Americano (`americano.png`)
>
> **Prompt**: A professional, high-quality photograph of an iced Americano coffee in a tall, clear glass on a rustic wooden cafe table. The coffee has beautiful dark mahogany layers mixing with clear water and large, crystal-clear ice cubes. Subtle condensation on the outside of the glass. Soft side-lighting from a window creating warm reflections. Bokeh background of a cozy cafe. Premium food photography style.

### 2. Cappuccino (`cappuccino.png`)
>
> **Prompt**: A high-end close-up photograph of a hot Cappuccino in a ceramic white cup and saucer. Perfect latte art (rosette pattern) on thick, creamy microfoam. Light dusting of cocoa powder on top. Steam rising gently. Placed on a dark marble countertop. Cinematic lighting, sharp focus on the foam texture, warm and inviting atmosphere.

### 3. Latte (`latte.png`)
>
> **Prompt**: A premium lifestyle photograph of a hot Cafe Latte in a clear glass mug, showing the beautiful gradient layers of dark espresso at the bottom and creamy steamed milk on top. Delicate heart-shaped latte art. Beside the mug is a small sprig of lavender on a light-colored wooden table. Soft, airy, and bright morning light. Professional culinary photography.

### 4. Croissant (`croissant.png`)

A small bowl of gourmet butter on the side. Warm, golden hour lighting highlighting the texture and crunch. Background is a blurred bakery shelf. 8k, ultra-realistic.
